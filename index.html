The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh is a nation of immense potential, built on the resilience and determination of its people. As Chief Advisor, I am humbled by the opportunity to contribute to the development of our beloved country. Over the years, Bangladesh has made remarkable progress in various sectors—be it in poverty alleviation, women’s empowerment, or technological advancements. Our journey has not been without challenges. However, it is through challenges that we discover our true strength. Today, our focus is on fostering sustainable development that benefits every citizen. From improving education and healthcare systems to creating employment opportunities and addressing climate change, our vision is comprehensive and inclusive. Bangladesh’s future lies in its people—our farmers, workers, entrepreneurs, and youth. By providing the right environment and resources, we can unleash their potential to transform our economy into a model for the world. Let us work together to build a Bangladesh that is not only self-reliant but also a beacon of hope and innovation for others. Together, we will achieve prosperity that upholds the dignity and aspirations of all."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
